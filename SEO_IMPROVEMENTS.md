This document outlines all SEO enhancements implemented to position Happy Pills Pharmacy as Uganda's leading online pharmacy and improve Google search rankings for local and national keywords.

## 1. Technical SEO Optimizations

### Schema Markup (JSON-LD)
✅ **LocalBusiness Schema** - Identifies the pharmacy location and services
✅ **Pharmacy Schema** - Specifies medical business type with specialties
✅ **FAQ Schema** - Displays common questions directly in search results
✅ **Product Schema** - For individual medicine/product pages
✅ **AggregateRating Schema** - Shows customer ratings in search results

**Benefits:**
- Rich snippets in Google Search results
- Improved click-through rates (CTR)
- Better semantic understanding by search engines
- Eligibility for Google Knowledge Panel

### Sitemap & Robots.txt
✅ **XML Sitemap** (`/public/sitemap.xml`)
- Lists all major pages with priority levels
- Includes update frequency metadata
- Helps Google crawl and index all content

✅ **Robots.txt** (`/public/robots.txt`)
- Allows search engines to crawl main content
- Blocks bad bots (Ahrefs, Semrush, MJ12bot)
- Sets crawl delay for respectful indexing
- Points to sitemap location

**Benefits:**
- Faster indexing of new pages
- Better crawler efficiency
- Protection from aggressive bots
